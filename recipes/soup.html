Soup

Description

Tomato egg soup or also known by the name "cloudy soup" because of the effect when the egg white is added to the broth, making it cloudy. It is easily made with the two main ingredients, of course, egg and tomato. In case you still have no idea how the soup is made, below is the list of ingredients and our simple steps so you can make yourself a warm pot of soup with all the nutrients needed for the coming cold days.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Coriander
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Cut tomatoes to wedges
  2. Break eggs into bowl and whisk till egg white and egg yolk blended
  3. Boil water and put salt
  4. Put tomato wedges into boiling water and lower the flame, wait till water boiling again then add egg slowly while stirring to make it cloudy
  5. Wait for the water to boil again, add the seasoning, pepper and coriander
